Eastern Shore Energy, LLC is a community-based, wind project based on Maryland's Lower Eastern Shore. We pride ourselves on our commitment to open, two-way communication with the community. We invite you to find out more about who we are and how our project is progressing.


Eastern Shore Energy Reaches Preliminary Financing Agreement

Eastern Shore Energy, LLC (ESE) announced today they have reached a preliminary financing agreement with Benjamin Travis, COO of World Equity Alliance, Inc (WEA). Based upon this agreement, the WEA Energy Private Equity Fund, LP intends to invest, as a joint venture partner in the development for the ESE projects.
