The Advisory Board

Eastern Shore Energy, LLC has elected a dedicated advisory group of area community members who are all connected to agriculture. The advisory group, made up of four members, have all invested in the development along with their manager, National Wind, LLC.

We are excited and proud to bring a project of this magnitude to the Delmarva Peninsula area and to provide an excellent economic resource for the community. The board will serve to advise and inform on all the issues involved in the development process.

Our goal is to promote local participation and awareness of the project and to work closely with the landowners throughout every stage. We want to give the people involved the opportunity to influence the project in a positive way.
